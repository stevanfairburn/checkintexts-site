Checkintexts helps you schedule personal check-ins with friends and family so you can stay in touch consistently.

Email support: support@checkintexts.com

Download on App Store

How it works

  1. You write a message and choose a cadence.
  2. The app schedules a reminder on your device.
  3. At the scheduled time, the app opens your phone's message composer.
  4. You review the message and decide whether to send it.

The app does not send messages automatically.

FAQ

  • Does it send messages automatically? No.
  • Does it reply or continue conversations? No.
  • Are messages generated automatically? No.
